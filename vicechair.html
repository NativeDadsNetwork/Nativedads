Vice Chair

Mary Hummingbird Thompson

Mary hummingbird Thompson is NDN's vice chair and is a proud native woman and a staple in the native community in Sacramento Ca. She is a certified domestic violence counselor and Site Manager for TANF Yolo county. Mary's been a mentor for native families for numerous years in Sacramento and surrounding areas, she is also white bison trainer, Motherhood is sacred and Healthy Relationships facilitator, as well as being a loving mother.
